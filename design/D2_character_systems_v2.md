## THE PRIMER SEQUENCE PROBLEM

*Why specialization exists in a world where anyone can learn anything.*

In the first eighteen months after the Kowloon Paper, threading was chaos. Underground sequencers activated whatever clusters they could reach, in whatever order the primers allowed. The results were unpredictable. Some patients gained extraordinary capabilities. Some gained nothing. Some burned out on the table.

A former AGS researcher named Lien Dao identified the pattern. Working from a clinic beneath a desalination plant in the Strand, she noticed that patients who survived threading well shared a common feature: their primers had activated Kowloon clusters in a specific sequential relationship. Clusters that shared chromatin-remodeling pathways. Clusters whose protein outputs served as cofactors for each other. Clusters that, activated in the right order, built biological infrastructure that made the next activation safer and more effective.

She called them primer sequences. Three emerged from her data as dramatically more stable than random activation: the somatic sequence (body-first), the cortical sequence (mind-first), and the sympathetic sequence (systems-first). These three paths didn't limit what a Threaded individual could eventually learn — they shaped the cost gradient. Your primer sequence builds biological infrastructure optimized for certain capabilities. Developing skills that align with that infrastructure is natural and efficient. Developing skills that conflict with it means rebuilding architecture your body has already committed to — possible, but expensive.

The Directorate's AGS division later identified at least seven additional stable sequences, but those remain classified. The three that Lien Dao released into the underground became the foundation of how the Unbound threads its people. They persist today as the three paths a newly Threaded individual walks when they choose who they're going to become.

But "who they're going to become" is no longer an abstract question. The world has threats that demand specific responses. The classes that emerged from Dao's primer sequences have been shaped — by necessity, by loss, by the specific horrors that uncontrolled threading and institutional power inflict on a population — into three distinct answers to three distinct problems.

## CORE ATTRIBUTES (8)

These are the measurable biological parameters of a Threaded individual. They represent raw capacity before training refines it into skill.

### 1. SOMA
**Raw physical force**

Muscle fiber density, tendon strength, skeletal reinforcement, neuromuscular recruitment. High Soma means more force, more load, more momentum.

*Affects: Melee damage, carry weight, inventory capacity, knockback force, block stability.*

### 2. REFLEX
**Neural response speed**

The latency between perception and action. High Reflex means faster reactions, quicker movement initiation, and precision under pressure.

*Affects: Attack speed, dodge chance, critical strike chance, movement speed, action queue responsiveness.*

### 3. VIGOR
**Biological durability**

The body's ceiling for absorbing damage, resisting shock, and maintaining function under stress. High Vigor means more tissue that survives trauma, faster clotting, greater pain tolerance.

*Affects: Maximum HP, armor effectiveness, death penalty reduction, HP regeneration, resistance to damage-over-time.*

### 4. CORTEX
**Mental throughput**

Neural processing capacity for Expression channeling, environmental analysis, and cognitive multitasking. High Cortex means larger Strand pools, stronger Expressions, faster cooldowns.

*Affects: Expression damage/potency, Strand pool size, cooldown reduction, concurrent active Expressions.*

### 5. RESOLVE
**Cognitive stability**

The mind's resistance to disruption, overload, and external interference. Where Cortex is bandwidth, Resolve is signal integrity. High Resolve means staying coherent when everything is trying to knock you off balance.

*Affects: Crowd control resistance, healing received, Expression interruption resistance, maximum concurrent buffs/debuffs.*

### 6. ACUITY
**Sensory fidelity**

The resolution and range of augmented perception. High Acuity means seeing threats earlier, identifying materials precisely, and perceiving details that lower-resolution senses miss.

*Affects: Detection range, ranged accuracy, gathering yield, identification speed, trap detection, crafting quality.*

### 7. PRESENCE
**Biosocial influence**

The aggregate effect of augmented pheromonal output, vocal modulation, micro-expression reading, and autonomic empathy. High Presence means people trust you faster, pay you more, and tell you things they shouldn't.

*Affects: Vendor prices, faction reputation gain, party buff effectiveness, NPC dialogue options, persuasion.*

### 8. PHLUX
**Phenotypic deviation tolerance**

How far your expression patterns can deviate from the programmed primer sequence before producing errors. High Phlux means more variance — better crits, rarer loot, wilder crafting outcomes, and occasional unlocks your primer never targeted. Also means a noisier biological baseline: fluctuating iris patterns, asymmetric bioluminescence, involuntary micro-Expressions.

*Affects: Critical damage multiplier, loot quality, crafting experimentation, rare skill unlocks, death penalty variance.*

## CLASSES (3)

Each class is a primer sequence. Each primer sequence is a response.

### SENTINEL
**Primer Sequence: Somatic**
*The world keeps producing things that need to be stopped. The Sentinel is what stops them.*

#### The Threat They Answer

When a rogue Threaded individual with destabilized expression pathways tears through a sublevel market, killing indiscriminately because their pain receptors burned out three months ago and they can't feel anything anymore — someone has to stand in front of that. When the Directorate's HIB sends an extraction team to dismantle an underground clinic and the sequencer inside needs sixty more seconds to finish threading a patient — someone has to hold the door. When the Null Current's latest operation goes sideways and a civilian block becomes a crossfire zone — someone has to be the wall between the bullets and the people who can't take them.

That someone is the Sentinel.

The somatic primer sequence was originally designed for survival, not combat. Lien Dao built it for Strand laborers whose bodies were failing from industrial work — dockworkers, salvage operators, people who needed to absorb punishment that would kill an unaugmented human. The first somatic-threaded patients didn't think of themselves as fighters. They thought of themselves as durable.

The world made them fighters anyway. Durability, it turns out, is the prerequisite for everything the resistance needs most: the ability to absorb damage so others don't have to; the ability to hold a position when retreat means losing people; the ability to survive long enough for the Catalyst to find the opening or the Conduit to get the wounded back on their feet.

Sentinels are not invincible. They are *refusable*. They are the line you cannot cross without going through them first, and going through them takes longer than most threats can afford.

### CATALYST
**Primer Sequence: Cortical**
*The Directorate built a system designed to contain everything. The Catalyst is the thing it can't contain.*

#### The Threat They Answer

The Directorate's power is structural. Atmospheric scanners. Biometric checkpoints. HIB enforcement squads with Authorized-grade threading and military hardware. The system is designed to make resistance futile through overwhelming, omnipresent control — a machine so large and so thorough that opposing it feels like opposing weather.

The Catalyst exists because weather can be broken.

The cortical primer sequence was the most dangerous path. It built systems the body had never used — electromagnetic transduction, ion channel proliferation, energy projection pathways encoded in Kowloon clusters that had been silent since before recorded history. Early cortical threading had a forty percent burnout rate. The survivors could perceive and project electromagnetic energy, disrupt electronic systems, and generate forces that conventional physics said a biological organism shouldn't be able to produce.

The Directorate recognized the threat immediately. Its own Catalysts — though it never uses that word — are the most operationally dangerous people on the planet: AGS-trained, precision-sequenced, and deployed when the Directorate needs something not just stopped but *unmade*. The only reliable counter to a Directorate Catalyst is an Unbound one.

Catalysts are the resistance's offensive edge. Where the Sentinel holds the line, the Catalyst is the reason the line moves forward. They are the chain reaction the system cannot contain, the discharge that finds the gap in the armor, the force that turns the Directorate's own infrastructure against it. They are fragile compared to Sentinels — their neural architecture prioritized power over resilience — but what they lack in durability they compensate for in the ability to end fights before durability matters.

### CONDUIT
**Primer Sequence: Sympathetic**
*Everyone else fights the war. The Conduit makes sure there's someone left to win it.*

#### The Threat They Answer

Threading has a cost. The cost is paid in bodies.

Underground clinics operate without AGS-grade equipment. Primers sourced from the black market carry impurities. Burnout kills people who trusted the wrong sequencer. Combat injuries in Threaded individuals present complications that no conventional hospital can treat — tissue that regenerates along the wrong template, neural pathways that short-circuit under stress, expression cascading triggered by physical trauma. And the Directorate doesn't offer medical coverage to Conditional citizens.

The Conduit exists because without them, the resistance bleeds out.

The sympathetic primer sequence was Lien Dao's last design — built specifically to produce someone the Directorate couldn't easily frame as a weapon. It targets Kowloon clusters governing cellular regeneration, biochemical synthesis, and autonomic nervous system modulation. A Conduit reads biological systems the way an engineer reads schematics: perceiving cellular stress, mapping tissue damage, identifying pathogenic load at resolutions no medical instrument matches. Then they intervene — directing their own biochemistry to synthesize targeted compounds and delivering them through contact or short-range biosignal transfer.

But the Conduit is not a medic standing at the back. The sympathetic sequence's deep integration with autonomic nervous systems means they perceive the biological state of everyone around them — heart rates, cortisol levels, inflammatory markers, expression pathway stability. They process this in real time and respond in real time: cycling between mending damaged tissue, projecting protective barriers, amplifying allies' biological output, and purging toxins and debuffs. A good Conduit is never doing just one thing. They are the heartbeat of the group — constant, rhythmic, cycling between functions faster than any other class cycles between abilities.

The Directorate was slow to recognize the sympathetic sequence's threat potential. Healers seemed benign. It took AGS eighteen months to realize that the same intercellular signaling that lets a Conduit repair biological systems also lets them *disrupt* them. A person who understands how to accelerate tissue repair understands how to reverse it. The Directorate now classifies sympathetic threading at the same threat level as cortical.

In the Unbound, Conduits occupy a protected role. They are the last evacuated when a clinic falls. They are never asked to fight unless they volunteer. They are also the most politically valuable asset the resistance has — a Conduit embedded in a Baseline community provides medical care that no Directorate facility offers to Conditional citizens, and every patient they treat is a potential ally.

#### Role: Healer / Support (Rotation-Based)
Sustains groups through constant skill cycling: heal → protect → amplify → cleanse → heal. Rewarded for maintaining rotation rhythm rather than camping in one mode. Not defenseless — sympathetic Expressions can disrupt biological systems as well as repair them — but offensive capability is secondary.

#### Starting Equipment
Medical wrap (cloth armor, antiseptic-lined), diagnostic band (wrist-mounted biosensor), contact gloves (augmented dermal transfer pads), chelation capsule (single-use debuff cleanse).

#### Passive Tree Entry
Healing potency and Strand efficiency nodes. Early branches toward group healing range, rotation tempo bonuses, and defensive channeling.

**Balance notes:**
- Each class has exactly 4 Native skills — enough identity without constraint.
- Sentinel has the most Opposed (5): somatic commitment makes mental/energy disciplines hardest to retrofit.
- Conduit has the fewest Opposed (2): sympathetic awareness makes most disciplines at least partially accessible.
- Catalyst sits between: powerful but specialized, cortical architecture conflicts with physical combat but not utility or support.
- All utility skills are Neutral for at least two classes. No one is locked out of core gameplay.
- Engineering is universally Neutral. Technology doesn't care about your primer sequence.
